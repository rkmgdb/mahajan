History of College

Our college is working under the direct control of D.A.V. College Managing Committee, New Delhi. Dr. Punam Suri, the President. D.A.V. College Managing Committee, New Delhi is our guiding spirit. Sh. R.S. Sharma, General Secretary. Dr. Satish Sharma, Director (Colleges) and at the same time our local Advisory Committee has been guiding and supporting this college morally, Socially and financially and we hope to receive the same in future also. This college was initially a ‘Girl’s’ Institution but in 2002, it was converted into a co-education college. It is located in the heart of city. The campus is well planned with modern facilities. It has well equipped Computer lab., Home Science lab, Music Room and well stocked Library. The college imparts education in Humanities and also offers master’s degree in History and Punjabi
